STA. LUCIA LAND INC. - Corporate business profile

Sta. Lucia Land, Inc. is a leading Philippine developer of residential communities and listed on the Philippine Stock Exchange (stock code—SLI). It is part of the Sta. Lucia Group with over 50 years track record of real estate development. The Sta. Lucia Group has completed over 220 projects and developed over 10,000 hectares of land across 70 cities and municipalities in 10 regions & 15 provinces across the Philippines. SLI’s portfolio consists of developments that include residential, commercial, leisure and retail assets.
